what we do

The ASCEND Project is a partnership between Blue Economics, SOBRO and Abyssinian Development Corp to provide the residents of NYC help to find a better job or career and start new businesses and expand existing ones. Users will find government data about different job types and future outlooks, real feedback and answers from experts, online courses, links to additional resources and contacts to government agencies and non-profits at no cost. As a living project, feedback from users will help grow the content on the site.

The ASCEND Project is developed by Blue Economics.

Blue Economics is a professional volunteer organization that was founded in 2010 with the idea that people can come together and use their talents to uplift their communities. BE leverages the skills, experiences, knowledge, and networks of its members to develop solutions to issues and problems that facilitate the economic advancement of individuals and communities.
